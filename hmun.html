Hanoi Model United Nations Organization (HMUN Org.) is a student-run organization bringing together like-minded individuals who share a passion for contemporary global issues and international relations. Located in Hanoi, the capital city of Vietnam, HMUN operates year-round with numerous activities revolving around Model United Nations, including the annual Main conference in July, Mock Model UNs, Training Sessions, and Community service activities.
